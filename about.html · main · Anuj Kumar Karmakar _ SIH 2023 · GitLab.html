<h2>What is Lawsy?</h2>
117 
<p>Lawsy is a law based quiz application that aims to increase the legal literacy rate in youth from the age of
118 
8 to
119 
16. It helps them to learn about their rights and responsibilities, and how to stand for themselves and by
120 
others.</p>
121
122 
<h2>What are the main features of Lawsy?</h2>
123 
<p>Lawsy has three main features:</p>
124 
<ul>
125 
<li>Quiz - The Quiz will help you to gauge your learning and test your knowledge on various legal topics.</li>
126 
<li>Learning Module - The Learning Module will provide you with interactive and engaging content to help you
127 
learn more about the law.</li>
128 
<li>Forums - The Forums will connect you with experts, NGOs, and government organizations that can provide
129 
you with guidance and support.</li>
130 
</ul>
131
132 
<h2>How does Lawsy support native languages?</h2>
133 
<p>Lawsy supports native languages by providing you with the option to choose your preferred language from a
134 
list of available languages. You can also switch between languages at any time. Lawsy uses audio-visuals and
135 
storytelling to make the content more accessible and understandable for different languages.</p>
136
137 
<h2>How does Lawsy provide incentives to encourage learning?</h2>
138 
<p>Lawsy provides incentives to encourage learning by rewarding you with points, badges, and certificates for
139 
completing quizzes and learning modules. You can also compete with other users on leaderboards and
140 
challenges. You can redeem your points for various rewards such as books, vouchers, or donations.</p>
